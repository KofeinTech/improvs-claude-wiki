# Offboarding Process

When a team member leaves Improvs, the following must happen. Create a Jira ticket in the IMP project with label `offboarding` -- automation will generate these subtasks.

## Access Revocation Checklist

- [ ] Revoke GitHub organization access
- [ ] Revoke Jira access
- [ ] Remove Claude organization seat
- [ ] Disable Google Workspace account (@improvs.com email)
- [ ] Revoke Figma access (if applicable)
- [ ] Remove from Apple Developer team
- [ ] Remove from Google Play Console
- [ ] Remove from Telegram work groups

## Knowledge Transfer

- [ ] Transfer ownership of their open Jira tickets to another team member
- [ ] Document any undocumented project knowledge in the wiki (projects/ section)
- [ ] Verify that all their code is committed and pushed (no local-only branches)
- [ ] Review their recent PRs for any pending work

## Equipment

- [ ] Collect company equipment (if applicable)

## Timeline

- Access revocation: completed by the last working day
- Knowledge transfer: completed during notice period
- Equipment return: within 5 business days of last day

## Who is responsible

- HR (Alina) creates the Jira ticket
- CEO verifies all access is revoked
- PM ensures knowledge transfer for affected projects
